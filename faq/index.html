CONFERENCE

When is SugarCon 2016?

SugarCon 2016 will kick off with a Welcome Reception on Monday, June 13th and end on Thursday, June 16th.

What is SugarCon?

SugarCon 2016 is SugarCRM's annual conference for customers, partners, developers and technology experts to explore the latest CRM trends and innovations. The conference includes three days packed with keynotes, breakout sessions and networking opportunities, including the SugarCon Conference Party.

Who should attend?

If you’re looking to improve business performance by building extraordinary relationships with your customers, you should attend SugarCon 2016. The conference will be a dramatic first step toward business transformation.

Of course, if you’re responsible for the use, management, or adoption of the SugarCRM Platform in your organization, SugarCon 2016 just can’t be missed. It’s the place to learn about the latest product enhancements and best practices for using CRM to transform your business.

Need to brush up on your skills? Register to attend SugarCRM training sessions led by SugarCRM University's expert trainers.

REGISTRATION

How much does it cost to attend SugarCon 2016?

Early Bird registration: $795
Standard registration: $995
Buy 4 passes / Get 1 pass free: Purchase four (4) full conference passes at the standard registration rate, get one (1) full complimentary conference pass at no charge.

What is included with my registration fee?

Your SugarCon registration fee includes access to the conference, conference materials, access and food at the Welcome reception on June 13 and SugarCon Party on June 14. Admission to the three days of the conference, featuring presentations by SugarCRM customers, industry analysts and SugarCRM executives, access to the partner pavilion and endless networking opportunities. Breakfast and lunch on both days, plus food during the evening networking functions.

Can I purchase a conference pass at the event?

Onsite registration is not available. We want to make every effort to be prepared for you when you arrive at SugarCon 2016, therefore we ask that you register before June 1, 2016.

What is the registration substitution and cancellation policy?

Cancellation Policy:

If you must cancel your registration for any reason, please notify us in writing by April 15, 2016 for a refund of your registration fees paid, less a $100 processing fee. A request for cancellation must be provided in writing by email to sugarcon2016@sugarcrm.com and the cancellation will be effective upon receipt by SugarCRM, provided that such written notice of cancellation is received by April 15, 2016. Cancellations within 60 days of the first day of the Event are non-refundable, and paid attendees who do not attend or who cancel after the deadline of April 15, 2016 are liable for the entire fee.


Transfer Policy:

You may transfer your registration to another person without incurring additional fees, if the requested transfer is 30 or more days from the first day of the Event, by providing a written request which includes proper authorization from the transferor to transfer its paid registration to an identified transferee, to sugarcon2016@sugarcrm.com. However, if the requested transfer is received within 30 days of the first day of the Event, such request will require payment of a $50 transfer fee.


Other Terms & Conditions:

In the unlikely event of cancellation of the Event, the sole and maximum liability of SugarCRM is limited to a full refund of paid registration fees received by SugarCRM.

TRAINING

What is included in Training?

Hands-on product training with Sugar University experts. Training consists of two half-day courses on Thursday, June 16. Register now — seats are limited!

Topics includes:

  • Creating Reports
  • Working in Studio
  • Emailing in Sugar
  • Managing Users, Teams, and Roles

Do I need to bring my computer for training courses?

Yes, please bring your computer and we'll provide everything else.

Is there a separate fee for the SugarCRM Training on Thursday, June 16?

Yes, all training and related materials are separate from the conference registration fee. Training is only open to those who have registered for the SugarCon Conference. Space is limited and allocated on a first registered first serve basis. Note: Space is limited to 25 attendees per class.

What things are there to do in San Francisco?

San Francisco is a place you can always find something to do or see. To check for local events happening during your stay or to explore local neighborhoods, restaurants and sights, check http://www.sanfrancisco.travel for the most up to date visitor information. Whether city sightseeing, a China Town culinary tour, a jazz concert, a visit to Alcatraz or Ghirardelli Square, or a tour of San Francisco MOMA, you can find the complete range of activities.

What is the expected weather in San Francisco in June?

San Francisco weather can be unpredictable and can change from block to block! The month of June is characterized by daily highs around 66°F and low temperatures around 52°F. We recommend packing with layers: shirts, sweaters, pants, and a jacket to allow for varying temperatures.
