Got customers? This is the conference for you.

Keynote Speaker

One of the world’s most cited experts on the Future of Customer Experience, Dr. Catriona Wallace will highlight exciting new trends in the evolution of CRM, including the rise of Conversational Commerce and new business models for generating shared value.

Dr. Wallace is the founder of Intelligent Guided Selling software company Flamingo; customer experience design firm, Fifth Quadrant; market research firm, ACA Research; and a women entrepreneur’s co-working space, The Ventura.

Featured Sessions

This year’s agenda is jam packed with highly informative sessions from executives, thought leaders, and SugarCRM product experts, including in-depth stories of CRM success from: Agfa, HTC, IBM, Marathon Sports, Mazars, Mitsubishi/Suzuki Brazil, Postnord, Sennheiser, Sherwin-Williams, Unifin, and more…

Customer journey expert, Phil Winters, will discuss Transforming the Agent Experience, and Paul Greenberg, Author of “CRM at the Speed of Light,” will unlock the secrets of Selling the Business Value of CRM. SugarCRM technical experts will go in-depth on product roadmap and the latest Sugar capabilities. Check out the Agenda page for more detail.

Learn, network and have some fun with the growing SugarCRM community.

Discover how to put these new technologies to use to drive growth, improve efficiency, and boost customer satisfaction and loyalty.
