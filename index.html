June 13-16, San Francisco
It's Time to Transform Relationships

REGISTER

New digital technologies are re-drawing the business landscape. It’s time to transform or be left behind. And it all starts with the customer perspective.

At SugarCon 2016, you’ll learn from experts and industry leaders how to take a holistic approach to Transform Relationships, aligning strategy, people, processes and systems to drive deeper customer understanding and deliver more engaging customer experiences.

Presenters include executives from:

Keynote Speaker

One of the world’s most cited experts on the Future of Customer Experience, Dr. Catriona Wallace will highlight exciting new trends in the evolution of CRM, including the rise of Conversational Commerce and new business models for generating shared value.

Dr. Wallace is the founder of Intelligent Guided Selling software company Flamingo; customer experience design firm, Fifth Quadrant; market research firm, ACA Research; and a women entrepreneur’s co-working space, The Ventura.
