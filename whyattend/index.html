Why Attend?


Got Customers? This is the conference for you.


Hear from Clint Oram, SugarCRM Co-Founder and CTO about what will make SugarCon great this year.

Check out highlights from last year’s SugarCon.

Transform Relationships

Industries are being disrupted by companies that are mastering digital and mobile technologies to create better connections and deliver greater value to customers. SugarCon 2016 will offer new insight from experts and industry leaders on how companies can take a holistic approach to Transform Relationships, aligning strategy, people, processes and systems to drive deeper customer understanding and deliver more engaging customer experiences.



Catch the Vision

Insightful Keynotes, the Sugar product roadmap, and sessions on strategic business transformation will reveal future Sugar enhancements, and how they can help you transform your business.



Build your Network

SugarCon will offer lots of great networking opportunities – with industry leaders and influencers, peers who share your interests and challenges, SugarCRM executives, technical experts and partners, and of course our event sponsors. Birds of a Feather sessions will offer opportunities to connect with attendees around topics that matter most to you.



Build your Sugar Expertise

We’ve created a whole new track called Sugar In-Depth where Sugar experts get into the nitty gritty on how to use new Sugar tech, like the Mobile SDK, Sugar 7.7, and Advanced Workflow. SugarCon also offers a hands-on “UnCon” track for developers, opportunities to interact with the SugarCRM engineering team in the SugarLabs, and Sugar University Training and Certification.



And Have Fun Too!

You won’t want to miss this year’s SugarCon Party and other fun events at the conference. Turn your trip into a getaway – the San Francisco Bay Area is a fantastic June travel destination.
